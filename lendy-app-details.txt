LENDY - LOAN TRACKER APP
=========================
"Track your loans, payments, and interest"

Website: https://johnvincenttalam.github.io/lendy/
Platform: Progressive Web App (PWA) - works on any device with a browser
Tech Stack: React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Zustand


WHAT IS LENDY?
==============
Lendy is a free, mobile-first loan tracking app that helps people manage
multiple loans, track payments, and plan their debt payoff journey. It works
entirely offline with no sign-up required - all data stays on the user's device.


WHO IS IT FOR?
==============
- People juggling multiple loans (cash loans, gadget installments, etc.)
- Anyone who wants to see a clear picture of their total debt
- Users who want to compare payment strategies to save money
- People in the Philippines where flat/simple interest lending is common
- Anyone who wants a simple, private way to track loan payments


CORE FEATURES
=============

1. TWO LOAN MODES
   - Standard Mode: For loans with interest rates (cash loans, personal loans).
     Enter the amount, monthly interest rate, tenure, and monthly payment.
   - Installment Mode: For gadget/appliance loans where the monthly payment
     already includes interest. Just enter the monthly payment and number of
     months - the app calculates the total automatically.

2. PAYMENT TRACKING
   - One-tap "Mark as Paid" button (sticky at the bottom for easy access)
   - Confirmation dialog showing payment breakdown before marking
   - Full repayment schedule showing every month's payment
   - Principal and interest breakdown per payment (for interest-bearing loans)
   - Visual status indicators: Paid, Next, Due
   - Undo option if you accidentally mark a payment

3. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
   - Total active loans count
   - Total remaining debt across all loans
   - Total monthly payment obligation
   - Total interest across all loans
   - Projected debt-free date
   - Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio with color-coded health indicator
   - Search, filter (All/Active/Paid), and sort (6 options) for loans

4. DEBT PAYOFF TIMELINE CHART
   - Interactive line chart showing how debt decreases over time
   - Hover/touch to see the balance at any specific month
   - Toggle to show or hide the chart
   - Motivational tool to visualize progress

5. PAY SCHEDULE
   - Calendar view grouped by half-month periods (1-15 and 16-31)
   - Shows which loans are due in each period and how much total
   - Swipe between periods on mobile
   - Quick way to see upcoming payment obligations

6. PAYMENT STRATEGIES
   - Compare three strategies side by side:
     * Current: Pay each loan as scheduled
     * Snowball: Pay smallest balance first (for motivation)
     * Avalanche: Pay highest interest first (saves the most money)
   - Adjustable extra monthly payment slider
   - Shows total months, total interest, total paid, and savings for each
   - Highlights the best strategy with a badge
   - Educational tool helping users optimize their payoff plan

7. LOAN DETAILS
   - Large remaining balance display with progress bar
   - Interest overview: total interest, total repayment, interest paid/remaining
   - Info grid: amount, monthly payment, months paid, start/end dates, total paid
   - Full repayment schedule with all payments listed
   - Edit and delete options

8. SECURITY & PRIVACY
   - Optional 4-digit PIN lock with SHA-256 hashing
   - Auto-locks when switching apps or tabs
   - All data stored locally on the device - no servers, no cloud, no tracking
   - Complete user control over their data

9. PAYMENT REMINDERS
   - Browser notification reminders before payments are due
   - Configurable: 1, 2, 3, 5, or 7 days before due date
   - Non-intrusive, once-per-day check

10. DATA MANAGEMENT
    - Export to CSV for spreadsheet use
    - Full JSON backup with one click
    - Restore from backup file
    - No vendor lock-in - your data is always yours

11. PROGRESSIVE WEB APP (PWA)
    - Install on your home screen like a native app
    - Works offline after first visit
    - No app store needed
    - Lightweight and fast

INTEREST CALCULATION MODEL
===========================
Lendy uses flat/simple interest calculation, which matches how informal and
micro-finance lending works in the Philippines:

  Total Interest = Principal x Monthly Rate x Number of Months
  Monthly Interest Portion = Total Interest / Number of Months
  Monthly Principal Portion = Principal / Number of Months

This is different from compound interest used by banks in many countries.
Each monthly payment is split into a fixed principal and fixed interest portion,
making it easy to understand exactly where your money goes.
